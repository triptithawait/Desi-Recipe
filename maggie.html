Maggie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggie noodles
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped (optional)
  • 1 small tomato, chopped (optional)
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste (optional)
  • Maggie masala (from the packet)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, bring 1 ½ cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add the Maggie noodles into the boiling water.
  3. Sprinkle the Maggie masala and stir well.
  4. Let the noodles cook for 2-3 minutes until the water is absorbed and the noodles are soft.
  5. For extra flavor, you can sauté onions, tomatoes, and green chili in oil or butter before adding the noodles.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with coriander leaves if desired.
