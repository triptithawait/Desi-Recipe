Maggie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1 small cucumber, grated or finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 small tomato, chopped (optional)
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Â½ tsp black salt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add the grated cucumber, tomato, onion, and green chilies (if using) to the yogurt.
  3. Add roasted cumin powder, black salt, and regular salt. Mix everything together.
  4. Let it sit in the fridge for a few minutes before serving
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve chilled.
